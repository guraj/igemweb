Experimental & Ideation Teams

We have several wetlab engineering and computational biology teams, each dedicated to addressing a distinct biological research question. Our experimental teams continue work on well-defined projects, while our ideation teams focus on exploring novel research areas. Joining one of our project teams helps build your technical skillset, ability to work as a team, and passion in bioengineering research.

Regardless of your experience level, check out our teams and consider applying to an Experimental or Ideation Team today before applications close on September 9 at 11:00 a.m.

External & Internal Relations Team (Eternals)

E-ternals is our combined External and Internal relations team. If you are a driven student with an affinity for both research science and the humanities, you're a great fit for Eternals.
We hope you'll join our team if you're interested in: hosting research events like the Cal Undergraduate Bioengineering Symposium (CUBS); graphic design and web development; forming partnerships with the biotech industry and academia; empowering underrepresented students with their first research opportunity; enhancing our growing community by organizing your own social events.

Sign up for an interview and (for folks passionate about graphic design) submit examples of any past design work by September 10 at 11:59 p.m.
