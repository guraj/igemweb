The UBER System

Adding a piece to the established UBER system, we provide a proof of concept for how UBER can be applied even more universally to organisms that are once difficult to tackle due to a lack of a library with promoters of different strengths. Our project shed light on how future researchers can build upon the UBER framework using our screening workflow. For the iGEM community, we contribute 52 distinct promoter sequences for the J23100 promoter family. Synthetic biology researchers can utilize our library to construct genetic expression designs on different transcriptional strengths.
