Our Demographics

iGEM at Berkeley is the largest student-run research organization on campus, and we see this as a responsibility to be uncompromising on diversity and inclusion. Accessbility is at the heart of our organization, and we're proud of the progress we've made to make research within reach for students from underrepresented and nontraditional backgrounds while acknowledging the work that remains.

These results are from our completely anonymous, voluntary demographic survey, covering a large sample size of our 400+ member organization. This survey was conducted in the fall of 2021.
